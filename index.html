What is BLEXCE

What is BLEXCE?

BLEXCE is a multinational company of unparalleled industry experts. BLEXCE is composed of young people who are highly motivated, exceptionally qualified, and passionate about their work. We have experts in Engineering (Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, Geomatic, Materials, Robotics), Medicine, Finance, Climate Change, Business Management, Computer Science (Software Engineering, Systems Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Automation, Cyber Security), Project Management, Demand Creation, Health (Medicine, Nursing, Public Health, Pharmacy, Physiotherapy), Monitoring and Evaluation, Construction, Agriculture, Economics, Law, Banking, Policy Specialists, Systems Analysts and others.

Who is BLEXCE

Who is BLEXCE

All the members of BLEXCE are from Africa - Zambia, Kenya, Botswana, South Africa, Malawi, Namibia and Zimbabwe. With several years' experience, educated by the best institutions in the world, the members have a track record to show they can not only get the job done, but get it done well. Our industry experts are allocated to BLEXCE subsidiaries based on their expertise and these subsidiaries will run their projects independently. Since our industry experts are divided by expertise, we guarantee quality service delivery every time. If you are reading this, you have been carefully selected because I know the value you bring to the table, excellence and passion above all else.

Why BLEXCE?

In 2012 I worked for a Russian company as a Software Developer and was paid 300 USD per month only to find out the projects that took me three months to work on were worth over 100,000 USD. African professionals get grossly underpaid for the work they do. The companies we work for abstract the true value of projects and pay us peanuts.


By Starting BLEXCE, I'm giving the opportunity to you to do what you love, but this time get paid what you're worth. We will work with a glass-door policy where everything is transparent. For all the projects we work on, 30% is shared by the shareholders/directors based on the percentage share they hold and 70% will be shared by the people that actually worked on the projects. Consultants may be hired on a needs basis and will be paid only for the projects they work on.


I know you all personally, I know you are smart, you are good at what you do, you are excellent! I don't want to hire you to work for me, I want us to own the business together, work together. When you own a business, you want it to succeed. I want us to succeed together.


Some of you are overlooked because of your lack of qualifications. You have the experience and the track record but employers constantly cite your lack of qualifications as an excuse to pay you peanuts. Not at BLEXCE, you get paid for the work you put in regardless of qualifications. To enable you to stop being disrespected outside BLEXCE, 500 of the 10,000 shares will be reserved for BLEXCE Education. With each project we run, 500 shares worth of the money will go to the education fund. If any of the Directors/Shareholders decides to pursue further studies, BLEXCE Education will sponsor them.


You're probably thinking, we don't have any projects yet. This company is worth nothing right? Yes, but once registered, BLEXCE profile will kick every proposal out of the water. There isn't another collection of so many smart, young, qualified, passionate people and our past track record speaks for itself. We are BLEXCE, we are excellent, we have been excellent all our lives but now we are working together to take on huge projects.


What will BLEXCE work on?

We will take on local and international projects with no limitations on borders. This will include but is not limited to; Tenders, Projects, Investments, Idea Development, System Development. We will work locally and export our expertise. To keep our minds moving, every director will have to bring to the table one business proposal each year. We will be divided into four groups so every quarter, we have some directors proposing their business ideas. If your idea is picked, 5% of the returns from that business will go to the one who brought the idea to the table. This incentivises you to bring ideas to the table that are actually worth our time.




How to be BLEXCE?

Thanks to COVID-19, we have found ways to work remotely in various fields so 80% of the work will be remote. You don't have to quit your job, BLEXCE will pay your for the work you do. There are three possible roles in BLEXCE; Consultant, Director/Shareholder, or Silent Investor.


BLEXCE Consultant

You don't buy any shares, instead you are contacted on a need basis. This means, if the internal BLEXCE team of directors requires your expertise, we will contact you with an offer for your work. With every BLEXCE project, 70% is reserved to manage the project and pay those who work on it, so your pay will come from this 70%.


BLEXCE Director/Shareholder

As a lot of people are busy with their jobs and other projects, you don't always have to be working on projects. For every BLEXCE project we have 30% of the value is shared amongst the shareholders based on the percentage share that each shareholder has. In addition, if you do work on a project, you also get paid for your work from the 70% project team allocation. Your share of the 70% will be based on your actual input to the project.


Silent Investor

A silent investor is someone who is busy and not available to work on any projects but believes in the potential of BLEXCE. As a silent investor, you can buy shares and for each BLEXCE project, 30% of it will be split amongst the shareholders based on the percentage share you each have. You don't get paid for actual project work as you don't work on projects. However, if at some point, you have some free time, you can work on projects as well and get paid your share from the 70% project fund.


How Much is the share price?

There are 10,000 BLEXCE shares, 500 of which have been reserved for the Education fund and 1000 for BLEXCE administrative costs, so only 8,500 shares are available which is 85% of BLEXCE. At the launch meeting, those interested in being Directors/Shareholders and Silent Investors will discuss the price of a single share of BLEXCE, once this is agreed, the shares will be split equally and each director is given time to pay for their shares. If you are unable to buy all the shares allocated to you, you can opt to get fewer shares in which case, the rest of the shares are put back in the pool and split equally amongst those who want as many shares as possible. Your shares make you a shareholder and director of BLEXCE and once the process is complete, we will register the company with your name listed as a shareholder, director, and beneficiary. BLEXCE will be registered in Zambia and all taxes paid to ZRA.
