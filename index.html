About Us

Blexce is a digital transport network platform where transporters with excess capacity on vehicles will link with would be parcel owners to move goods to required destinations. The main challenge that majority of individuals, small and medium enterprises face is unreliable, unpredictable transport costs to ferry goods from one place of supply to a place of demand. We promote efficiency in the economy as it will reduce information mismatch which leads to distortion of transport fares and consequent incessant price hikes.
